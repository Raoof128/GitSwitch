# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in the GitSwitch project and community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and collaborative.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while staying technically rigorous.
- Help keep discussions accessible to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained hostility
- Sharing private information without permission
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- Disruptive behavior that undermines productive collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact `raoof.r12@gmail.com`. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially as possible and handled with care.
